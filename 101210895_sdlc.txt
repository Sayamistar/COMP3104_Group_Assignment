DevOps is a set of practices that combines software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops). It aims to shorten the systems development life cycle and provide continuous delivery with high software
DevOps is complementary with Agile software development; several DevOps aspects came from Agile methodology.

Academics and practitioners have not developed a unique definition for the term "DevOps".[a][b][c][d]
